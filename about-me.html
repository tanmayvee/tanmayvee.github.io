About Me

I’m Mayvee Venus Tan — a visual storyteller, creative director, and editor driven by emotion, movement, and memory. My work thrives at the intersection of aesthetic and intimacy. From dreamy fashion cuts to poetic lifestyle montages, I build every piece to make people feel — and remember.

I started creating videos in my bedroom using whatever camera I could get my hands on, slowly developing a language rooted in softness, texture, and flow. That DIY spirit still fuels my work today, whether I’m scripting for creators, editing short films, or curating a brand’s visual identity. Every frame I touch has intention.

As someone deeply observant and detail-oriented, I pick up on the nuances of light, silence, and body language. That empathy guides how I shape narratives. I believe good editing isn’t flashy — it’s invisible. You don’t just see my edits; you feel them.

Outside of client work, I share slices of life on YouTube and Instagram, where I explore personal projects around travel, fashion, and culture. I’ve been honored to be selected as a member ofthe Vancouver Art Gallery Media Association and trusted by brands like Lovenote Bride for my vision. But at my core, I’m just a girl who loves the click of a timeline snapping into place.

My goal? To keep creating with feeling — always. Whether I’m editing in silence at 2 a.m. or laughing on set, I bring heart into every step.
