My Work

Through My Lens - Sep 3, 2023: Co-directed and edited this city of Vancouver feature, which explores the experience and life of a Vancouver tourist through Josh's eyes. I collaborated closely with Josh to bring out the identity of the city throughout his video, authentic moments and an informative voiceover narrative consistent with his content. This project has reached over 96k views, resonating with travel-focused audiences.

Gabby Le - Oct 6, 2023: Collaborated closely with Gabby on this Vancouver vlog, where we really focused on bringing her down-to-earth vision to life through editing and direction. Worked together to shape the pacing and flow of the video, making sure each moment reflected the genuine vibe of Vancouver while staying true to Gabby’s style. I directed the overall feel, ensuring the transitions and edits were smooth and aligned with the relaxed, real-life atmosphere we wanted to capture. As we edited together, I made sure every shot served the story, creating a natural flow that highlighted the fun of her experiences and Gabby’s authentic storytelling. This project has reached over 33.6k views, with many positive comments from her audience.

My YouTube Channel: I create and edit lifestyle and fashion content that’s all about sparking new ideas and making style feel fun, not intimidating. I’m genuinely passionate about the entire process — from brainstorming a concept at 11 p.m. to getting lost in editing until everything looks just right. I love taking an idea and turning it into something visual that makes people want to try something new or see fashion a little differently.

Lovenote Bride: Managed editing and social marketing for Lovenote, a boutique bridal brand with a branch in Vancouver. I made sure that output content aligned with Lovenote's branding, and worked closely with the team to create a cohesive visual identity focusing on the unique, marketable aspects of the brand and its product line. This included editing promotional videos, styling content for social media, keeping customers up to date on the latest designs and boosting brand visibility across their platforms.

Vancouver Art Gallery Media Association: Scouted and selected for the Media Association member program after gaining recognition for my content from my photoshoot at the Vancouver Art Gallery. I leveraged my network to spread the word about new exhibits and openings, sharing updates and behind-the-scenes content with my followers. Using social media storytelling to engage the community, I provided a personal and authentic perspective on the exhibit openings I attended. My content helped promote the gallery’s events while building a deeper connection between the art community and my audience.
